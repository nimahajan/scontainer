Quality

Maintaining quality of our products is our topmost priority.

Packaging Technologists know Shreeram Container stands for inspired design, consistent quality, dynamic service, flexibility - and the ability to come in on time and on budget. We are constantly researching new technologies and materials, and continuously improving our product portfolio.

Our commitment to the marketplace is to provide:

  • Best Quality

    • We pay attention to many factors in order to produce high class containers. Top quality containers can't be manufactured without quality raw materials. So we use HDPE blow and injection grade polymer granuals as raw materials from reputed suppliers like Indian Oil, Gail, Hadlia Petochemicals, Reliance Polymers.

    Addtionally, our QA team continuously watch for containers quality considering factors such as shape, weight and uniform color during production cycle. Samples are picked from batches for qulity inspection time to time. Even if small defect is found then defective container are sent to recycle process.

    • At shreeram container, we always try to understand customer requirements first. Several times, customers who are trying to launch their new products come to us for packaging need. Based on their requirements, we provide various container options to them. For requirement of building product family, we offer small containers from size 5 ml to big containers of 1Ltr size of similar shapes. That helps them to launch their product faster in market. Once customers see good response and growing demand to their product, we also help them in designing their own uniquely shaped products. We respect customer owned product designs and such containers are never delivered to any other customer. In today's competitive environment, we know the importance of having products in stores at right time. So we look for delivering customer orders in fastes possible way.
