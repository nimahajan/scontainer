About Us

Delivering packaging solutions since last 20 years.

Since 1996, Shreeram Container specialises in the production of high-quality plastic bottles. In a consumer-driven environment distinctive packaging is vital, and Shreeram Container offers unrivalled expertise in developing packaging solutions for markets where quality and style are critical to a product’s success. Markets such as pesticide bottles, pharmaceutical and tools packaging.

We have an extensive range of over standard products, and are also able to offer custom moulding of exclusive designs with a low cost of entry. Virtually everything is conducted in-house, on site - from concept design, through toolmaking to production, artwork and decoration.

Packaging Technologists know Shreeram Container stands for inspired design, consistent quality, dynamic service, flexibility - and the ability to come in on time and on budget. We are constantly researching new technologies and materials, and continuously improving our product portfolio.

Our aim is to deliver the fastest, full service, high-quality packaging in the industry. Our manufacturing unit is based in MIDC, Jalgaon, Maharashtra, where Shreeram Container employs 10 people on a custom-built 14000 sq. foot site, with excellent transport links to the main road network.
